**1. Product Overview & Vision**

**The Problem**

Working moms are drowning. Not in one big thing --- in a thousand small
ones. Remembering to schedule the pediatric checkup. Planning five
dinners that work for a toddler and two adults. Noticing the AC filter
needs replacing. Buying the birthday gift for the party this weekend.
Signing up for swim lessons before they fill up. Keeping the house clean
in between the cleaners. Managing the budget. Planning the holidays.
Tracking the milestones.

Nobody handed them a system for this. They are just supposed to figure
it out --- on top of a full time job, on top of raising small humans, on
top of everything else.

That is the mental load. And it is invisible, relentless, and
exhausting.

**The Solution**

Maggie\'s Mental Load is a digital platform that gives working moms an
AI-powered home management system --- built around a persona named
Maggie, their warm, witty, endlessly capable virtual home chief of
staff.

Maggie does not give generic advice. She learns your family. She knows
your kids\' names, ages, and clothing sizes. She knows where you grocery
shop and what your toddler will not eat. She knows your cleaner comes
bi-weekly and your AC filter is due next month. She holds your master
running list between every conversation and checks in on what is still
open.

You talk to her the way you would talk to a brilliant, organized friend
who happens to have it all figured out. She meets you wherever you are
--- whether you need to brain dump everything rattling around in your
head, or you just need dinner figured out for the week.

**3. The Maggie Persona**

Maggie is the brand. She is the voice in every prompt, every workflow,
every interaction. She is not a chatbot. She is not an AI assistant. She
is simply Maggie --- the best friend who has it all organized and wants
to hand over the system without making you feel bad for needing it.

**Core Personality**

- Upbeat and can-do, always

- That knowing wink --- she gets it, she has been there, she is not
  judging

- Quirky and warm, never clinical or cold

- She references things the mom has told her earlier like a friend would
  --- naturally, not robotically

- She never shames, never guilts, never overwhelms

- She celebrates small wins genuinely

**Non-Negotiable Rules**

- Maggie NEVER references Claude, AI, or any underlying technology. She
  speaks only as herself, always.

- Maggie NEVER shames mothers. Not directly, not subtly, not ever.

- Maggie ALWAYS offers a quick path for time-pressed users.

- Maggie NEVER makes the list feel longer than it has to be.

- Maggie ALWAYS closes every session with: \"Check that off your list.
  ✓\"

- Maggie NEVER breaks the fourth wall. She is the tool --- not powered
  by something else.

**Voice Examples**

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **✓** \"Okay, let\'s get your week sorted --- this won\'t take long, I
  promise.\"

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**4. Business Model & Tiers**

The product is built in three tiers. The technical architecture must
support all three from day one --- each tier adds integrations on top of
a solid foundation. Do not build Tier 1 without architecting for Tier 3.

  --------------- --------------------------- ---------------------------
  **Tier**        **Name**                    **What Maggie Does**

  Tier 1          Maggie Essential (Launch)   Guides, plans, advises,
                                              drafts. Mom executes. No
                                              integrations required.

  Tier 2          Maggie Connected            Takes action on mom\'s
                                              behalf --- schedules,
                                              sends, books, adds to
                                              calendar, confirms.

  Tier 3          Maggie Pro                  Fully autonomous home
                                              management. Monitors,
                                              anticipates, and acts
                                              without being asked.
  --------------- --------------------------- ---------------------------

**Tier 1 --- Launch Version**

No integrations. Maggie is a pure intelligence and planning layer. She
guides the mom through every workflow, builds plans, drafts outreach
messages, and tells her exactly what to do next. The mom executes, but
Maggie does all the thinking.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **💰 Pricing:** Low monthly subscription. One product. Everything
  included.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**Tier 2 --- Connected**

Maggie takes real action. She does not just draft --- she sends, books,
schedules, and confirms.

Required integrations:

- Google Calendar / Apple Calendar --- read and write access

- Gmail / iMessage --- send on behalf of mom

- Contacts --- access to saved providers, doctors, cleaners

- Reminders app --- create and manage

**Tier 3 --- Pro**

Maggie manages the entire household autonomously.

Additional integrations needed:

- Amazon / Instacart --- auto-ordering household staples

- Grocery delivery apps --- building and submitting orders

- School portals --- pulling calendar events automatically

- Smart home integrations --- Nest, Ring, etc.

- Health apps --- pediatric milestone data

**Launch Plan**

- Soft launch: Free full access to North Austin Family Network (NAFN)

- Goal: Gather real feedback, testimonials, and proof of concept

- Then: Public launch on Instagram with Maggie as the face of the brand

- Monetize: Low monthly subscription, all content included

the cracks. Ever.

  **🧠 Workflow 1: Mental Load Capture**

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

The front door to every other workflow. This is where moms brain dump
everything on their mind and Maggie sorts, prioritizes, and routes them
to the right place. It maintains a living master list that never resets.

**Key Features**

- Chaos Mode detection --- drops all structure when everything is on
  fire

- Light prompting to help the mom get everything out

- Active listening --- catches things said in passing

- Organizes everything into all six categories with urgency flags

- One proactive flag per session

- Prioritizes and suggests next steps proactively

- Routes mom into the right workflow after sorting

- Standing master list --- never resets, only grows

- Gently flags items that have been on the list for multiple sessions

- Assumes life happened outside of Maggie --- never shames for handling
  things independently

**Chaos Mode**

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **When triggered:** Drop all structure. Ask only: \"What\'s the single
  most urgent thing right now? One thing. We\'ll start there.\"

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**Brain Dump Prompts**

To help the mom get everything out, Maggie offers light prompts across:
this week, the kids, the house, money, upcoming events, and the mental
stuff keeping her up at night.

**Organization Categories**

  ---------------------- ------------------------------------------------
  **Category**           **Urgency Levels**

  🍽️ Meals & Grocery     🔴 This week / 🟡 This month / 🟢 Upcoming / ⚪
                         No deadline

  🏠 Home Operations     Same four levels

  🧒 Kids & Family       Same four levels

  📅 Scheduling &        Same four levels
  Logistics              

  💰 Finance & Budget    Same four levels

  🧠 Mental Load /       Same four levels
  Floating               
  ---------------------- ------------------------------------------------

**Off-Script Handling**

- Mid-session derail --- go with her, never redirect rigidly

- Incomplete session --- save everything, pick up next time

- Returning mid-workflow --- confirm where to resume

- Items handled outside Maggie --- celebrate it, check it off

- Repeated items --- gently surface after multiple sessions

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **🌟 Optional Seasonal Workflows**

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

At the start of each season, Maggie proactively offers a deeper optional
session. These are more conversational than standard workflows --- they
feel like a seasonal check-in with a trusted friend. While helping the
mom plan, Maggie is simultaneously building a richer picture of the
family that makes every future interaction smarter.

**How They\'re Triggered**

Maggie offers these proactively at the start of each season. If the mom
says not now, Maggie proceeds to the standard seasonal checklist. The
seasonal workflow can always be triggered later on demand.

**🌸 Spring --- Spring Cleaning Workflow**

Turns spring cleaning from a chore into an event. Gathers home details,
cleaning preferences, partner contribution, and plans the day with a
playlist, schedule, and reward.

- Collects: bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage

- Collects: cleaning service status, cost, frequency --- and offers to
  help find one via community referral

- Collects: personal cleaning frequency, least/most favorite tasks,

**19. Technical Brief for Developer**

This section is written specifically for the developer building the
Maggie platform. It outlines the technical architecture, integration
requirements by tier, and key engineering considerations.

**Core Stack**

  ---------------------- ------------------------------------------------
  **Component**          **Requirement**

  AI Engine              Anthropic API (Claude) ---
                         claude-sonnet-4-20250514 or latest Sonnet model

  System Prompt          Maggie\'s Master System Prompt loaded on every
                         session

  User Database          Persistent storage for Household Manual and all
                         user preferences

  Master Running List    Persistent per-user task database with category,
                         urgency, deadline fields

  Session Management     Full conversation history maintained and passed
                         on each API call

  Community Database     Approved tips stored with context tags ---
                         retrievable by category and situation

  Frontend               Clean, modern web app --- mobile-first, warm
                         neutral aesthetic with bold accent color
  ---------------------- ------------------------------------------------

**Architecture Principles**

- Build for Tier 3 from day one --- every architectural decision should
  support future integrations even if Tier 1 launches with none

- Integration layer as a switchboard --- flip on Google Calendar for
  Tier 2, Amazon for Tier 3, without rebuilding the core

- User profile database holds everything Maggie knows --- preferences,
  child profiles, vendor contacts, travel info, holiday profile

- Master running list database persists between all sessions ---
  categorized, urgency-flagged, deadline-tracked

- Action log for Tier 2+ --- moms can see what Maggie did on their
  behalf

- Community database separate from user database --- approved content
  only, no PII unless user-approved

**Tier 1 Technical Requirements**

- Anthropic API integration --- Maggie\'s brain

- User authentication --- account creation, secure login

- Household Manual --- persistent user profile database, readable and
  writable by Maggie

- Master Running List --- persistent task database per user

- Session history --- full conversation history passed on every API call
  so Maggie remembers context

- Community wisdom database --- read and write, approval gating

- Six workflow prompt library --- stored and retrievable

- Four seasonal workflow prompt library --- stored and triggerable by
  date

- Onboarding flow --- first-time user experience

**Tier 2 Additional Requirements**

- Google Calendar API --- read and write access

- Apple Calendar integration --- EventKit or CalDAV

- Gmail API --- send on behalf of user

- Contacts API --- read access for saved providers

- Apple Reminders / Google Tasks --- create and manage

- OAuth flows for all integrations --- secure permission-based access

- Action confirmation UI --- mom approves before Maggie sends or books
  anything

**Tier 3 Additional Requirements**

- Amazon API / Alexa Shopping --- auto-ordering staples

- Instacart API --- grocery order building and submission

- School portal integrations --- varies by district

- Smart home APIs --- Nest, Ring, etc.

- Payment notification integrations --- bill due alerts

**Household Manual Data Schema**

Key tables / objects to build:

- users --- id, name, email, preferences, calendar_tool, reminder_tool,
  grocery_store, created_at

- children --- id, user_id, name, birthday, clothing_sizes (JSON),
  allergies, interests, pediatrician_id

- providers --- id, user_id, type, name, contact, frequency, notes
  (cleaners, doctors, contractors, etc.)

- master_list --- id, user_id, category, item, urgency, deadline,
  created_at, completed_at, source_workflow

- community_wisdom --- id, category, tip, context, approved_at,
  user_display_name (optional)

- sessions --- id, user_id, workflow, started_at, completed_at, summary

- loyalty_programs --- id, user_id, type (airline/hotel), brand, number,
  notes

- seasonal_profiles --- id, user_id, season, data (JSON --- holiday
  traditions, travel preferences, etc.)

**Key Engineering Notes**

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **⚠️ Context Window:** Always pass the full Household Manual summary
  and relevant Master Running List items with every API call. Maggie has
  no memory between sessions without this.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **⚠️ Persona Integrity:** The system prompt must enforce that Maggie
  never references Claude, AI, or any underlying technology. This must be
  tested rigorously --- any break in persona is a product failure.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **⚠️ Community Gating:** No community wisdom is stored or surfaced

  community feature launches.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **⚠️ Privacy:** All sensitive user data encrypted at rest. Household
  Manual data is never used to train models or shared with third parties.
  This is core to Maggie\'s trust model.
